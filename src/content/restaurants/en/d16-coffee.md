A specialty coffee bar run by a local team of baristas and roasters, tucked into the heart of Diocletian's Palace on Dominisova. They roast their own beans — 100% arabica single origins to Specialty Coffee Association standards — and the espresso, flat whites and filter are dialled in with care, which makes it a genuine step up from the generic coffee on the tourist terraces nearby. The room is small and full of character, with fast WiFi and power outlets, so it doubles as a quiet laptop stop between sights. There's a short list of pastries to go with the coffee; for food, pair it with one of the konobas in our directory. A second D16 location sits on Trogirska, just outside the Palace. Hours shift with the season, so check before a special trip.
